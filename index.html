About

I'm an aspiring game artist trying to break into the industry. The hardest part of breaking into this industry is finding that all important first job! So far i've been caught up in a chicken & the egg problem where it is difficult to find a job with no prior experience, but it's impossible to gain that experience without a job.
 

I really enjoy working on game charcters, but i feel that i can draw just about anything. I tried to include a wide variety of art samples on my portfolio including charcters, environments and UI. I can't animate, but it's something that i would love to learn someday.
